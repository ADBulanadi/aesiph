Books

AESi Books is an accounting software solution for small to medium-sized business to manage most financially-related business processes - from Entering of Sales, Tracking of Expenses, Preparing Invoices, Purchase Order Processing, Inventory Management, up to generation of financial statement reports. We can also customize the process and workflow according to your business rules.

  • Financial Management
  • Customer Vendor Management
  • Sales and Expense Tracking
  • Intelligent Reporting
  • BIR Compliant VAT Management
  • Customizable Dashboard
