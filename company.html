Accur8 Enterprise Solutions Inc.

is a cloud based automation solutions provider for the logistics industry that offers leading-edge end-to-end automation systems for industrial terminals, Cargo & Logistics companies, Manufacturing companies and small to medium-sized enterprises (SME). It was founded in 2015 with just seven people with eager minds, full of passion and grit. Its founders capitalize on the principle that

"First and foremost, we must build solutions that create value to our customers."

At AESi, this is and will always be who we are.
