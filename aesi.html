AESi
Automation Solutions

is an Enterprise Resource Planning Suite in the Philippines designed to automate the supply chain management and logistics of Port, Transportation, Warehouse and Queueing operations of trucks and equipment.

As a fully flexible and configurable ERP, it is built around the need for business intelligence, giving you accurate insights you need to be able to focus on your core operational strategies.

We believe that the Philippines has a lot of room to grow. Being an archipelago, we want to maximize our ports as our gateway to economic growth. We created AESi Automation Solutions to revolutionize the way logistics operations are managed, and help drive solid results.

We built AESi to be

User Experience-Centric

We’ve spent a lot of time making AESi very intuitive and easy to use. Whether you’re on a PC, Mac, or a mobile device, you can fully utilize AESi, even with minimal to no training at all.

Accurate Business Intelligence

Our dashboards and reporting tools present data like you’ve never seen before. AESi provides actionable information to help you focus on developing strategies and concentrate on decision making.

Quality and Reliability

Behind AESi is a team of industry veterans, subject matter experts, business analysts, and senior developers. We ensure that our systems perform as intended because we want you to get a robust and reliable solution that works.

Flexibility and Configurability

We design, build and configure to your specific business requirements, which is why AESi can always adapt to your operations. Tell us what you need and we'll tailor-fit our systems for your operations.

Continuous Innovations

We understand that the business landscape continuously change due to the demands of the market and fast-paced technological advancement, and we commit that we will be your partner in bringing these advancements and solutions through our system improvements and upgrades.

Excellent Support

AESi is backed by our dedication to customer satisfaction. We’ll work with you every step of the way so that you get the most out of our systems.
